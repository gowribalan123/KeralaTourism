Wayanad

The name Wayanad is derived from Vayal Nadu, which means Paddy fields (Vayal) and land (Nadu).
Tourist Attractions: Edakkal Caves, Kuruva Dweep, Pookot Lake, Thirunelli Temple, Soochipara Falls, etc.
